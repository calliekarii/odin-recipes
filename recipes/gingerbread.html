Gingerbread Cookies

Description

This is my grandma's original recipe, tweaked by me over the years with the addition of molasses for an even more intense flavour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shortening
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup liquid honey
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 4 cups flour + extra for dusting
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tsp nutmeg

Steps

  1. Combine shortening and sugar in large bowl, beat until light and fluffy.
  2. Beat in honey, molasses, egg and vanilla.
  3. Sift flour, baking soda and spices together, add to the shortening mixture and knead well.
  4. Chill for several hours.
  5. Preheat oven to 300Â°F.
  6. Cut cookies into desired shapes and bake for 10 minutes.
  7. Transfer immediately to wire racks to cool.
