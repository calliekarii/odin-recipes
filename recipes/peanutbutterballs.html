Peanut Butter Balls

Description

A classic no bake recipe that's a little more difficult to make but worth the effort - just don't lick your fingers while making!

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 cups icing sugar
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 9 oz chocolate chips
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil

Steps

  1. Mix icing sugar, peanut butter, butter and vanilla, and form into smooth, round balls.
  2. Place a toothpick in each ball, place on waxed paper on a cookie sheet and refrigerate until hard.
  3. Melt chocolate chips and add coconut oil.
  4. Dip balls in melted chocolate mixture and return to cookie sheet, or stand on toothpicks to cool.
